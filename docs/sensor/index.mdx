# luchtmeter

This product consists of a measuring device that is linked to an app and keeps the user regularly informed by means of push notifications. Through AI, personalized suggestions are given to the user so that people can take actions and become aware of how they can optimize the air quality indoors and thereby contribute to health and well-being.
